About Paws & Claws Wildlife Rescue

Dedicated to giving wildlife a second chance since 2020

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by Dr. Sarah Jones and a team of passionate volunteers, Paws & Claws Wildlife Rescue was born from a critical need to provide specialized care for the Western Cape's vulnerable indigenous wildlife. What began as a small backyard operation has grown into a recognized rehabilitation center that has helped hundreds of animals return to their natural habitats.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide expert care and a second chance at life for South Africa's vulnerable wildlife through rescue, rehabilitation, and release, while promoting public awareness and coexistence between human and animal communities.

Our Vision

A future where human and wildlife communities thrive together harmoniously, with every injured or orphaned animal receiving the care they need to return to their natural habitat.
