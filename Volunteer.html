Volunteer Opportunities

We have various roles available for dedicated individuals who want to contribute to wildlife conservation. No experience is necessary for most positions - we provide full training!

Animal Care Assistant

Time Commitment: 4+ hours per week

Responsibilities:

  • Prepare food and feeding schedules
  • Clean enclosures and habitats
  • Assist with basic animal care under supervision
  • Monitor animal behavior and report changes

Rescue Transport Volunteer

Time Commitment: On-call basis

Requirements: Valid driver's license, own vehicle

Responsibilities:

  • Transport injured animals to our facility
  • Assist with rescue operations
  • Transport animals to veterinary appointments

Administrative Support

Time Commitment: Flexible hours

Responsibilities:

  • Answer phones and emails
  • Assist with fundraising campaigns
  • Help with social media and outreach
  • Data entry and record keeping

Facility Maintenance

Time Commitment: Flexible hours

Responsibilities:

  • Enclosure repair and maintenance
  • Grounds keeping
  • Equipment maintenance
  • General facility upkeep

Volunteer Requirements

  • Must be 18 years or older (16+ for supervised positions)
  • Commit to a minimum of 3 months of service
  • Complete our orientation and training program
  • Pass a basic background check
  • Have a genuine passion for wildlife conservation
  • Be physically able to perform required tasks
