## Project Overview
This project involves the development of a comprehensive website for Paws & Claws Wildlife Rescue, a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and releasing injured and orphaned indigenous wildlife in South Africa. The website serves as a digital hub for wildlife rescue operations, volunteer coordination, and public education.

## Website Goals and Objectives
- **Primary Goal:** Establish an online presence that facilitates wildlife rescues, volunteer recruitment, and public education
- **Specific Objectives:**
  - Increase online donation revenue by 25% within 6 months
  - Receive 5+ new volunteer applications weekly via website forms
  - Achieve top 3 Google search ranking for "wildlife rescue Cape Town"
  - Provide 24/7 access to emergency contact information
  - Educate the public about wildlife conservation
